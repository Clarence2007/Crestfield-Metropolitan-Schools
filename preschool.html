Biskila Adiche
Head of Preschool


The curriculum of the Preschool (grades playgroup through upper kindergarten) centers on the student’s understanding and development of the core subjects of arts, reading, phonics, and numeracy.

Our playgroup through upper-kindergarten program aims to prepare our youngest learners to become eager, curious, and inquisitive individuals with strong character and respect for all, ultimately making a positive impact on the world.

Based on research showing that children learn best through active, exploratory experiences, our Preschool curriculum is designed to offer days filled with exploration, investigation, and play.

Students learn in a warm and nurturing environment that emphasizes wonder and discovery. Every subject incorporates play, whether through challenging academics, interactive science activities, or social skills development. We focus daily on building a foundation of life skills, critical thinking, and a love of learning.


- Biskila Adiche | Head of Preschool
