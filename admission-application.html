ADMISSION APPLICATION PROCESS

In order to be enrolled in Crestfield, all prospective students must meet and comply with the criteria for admission. Prospective families are encouraged to schedule a school visit by contacting the Admissions Office. Most students are admitted at the beginning of the school year. However, due to the ebb and flow of the community, Crestfield has a rolling admission where we admit students all round provided they meet all the criteria for admission and there is avaialble class space.

Thank you very much for considering Crestfield as a schooling option for your child.

Please use the following links to access our application form & checklist which should be printed and filled and brought back to the school. You can also get the form at the school's admissions office.



Application Form

You will need to come with these supporting documents when applying for admission at the Admissions office. Supporting documents include:

  1. 1. Copy of Birth Certificate
  2. 2. 4 Passport-Sized Photographs
  3. 3. Immunization documents as required by the school at an accredited health centre/ hospital.
  4. 4. Previous Report Cards (For transfer students)


If you are in Abuja, and would like to schedule a visit, please do so by clicking on this conatct form linkto access the email directory where you can send a direct message to the main office and get a response in less than 24 hours .
