Head of Secondary School


The secondary school curriculum emphasizes students' understanding and development in key subjects like mathematics, cultural arts, science, national values, English, languages, religious studies, ICT, pre-vocational studies, and physical education.

Welcome to our secondary school, spanning from J.S.S.1 to J.S.S.2 momentarily, where our mission is to cultivate an enriching environment that fosters academic excellence, personal development, and social responsibility in each student.

Our secondary school program provides a holistic journey aimed at equipping students with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to thrive in an ever-evolving society. We prioritize nurturing the whole individual, valuing not just academic progress but also emotional resilience, social interactions, and personal growth.

Informed by years of educational research and best practices, our curriculum is meticulously crafted to offer a diverse array of learning experiences tailored to the unique needs and interests of our students. From dynamic lessons in mathematics and language arts to hands-on explorations in science and creative expression in the arts, every facet of our curriculum is designed to spark curiosity, foster creativity, and nurture critical thinking.

Central to our educational philosophy is the belief that active engagement drives optimal learning outcomes. Therefore, our classrooms are vibrant spaces where students are encouraged to inquire, explore, and connect their learning to the world around them. Through experiential learning endeavors such as field trips, interactive projects, and collaborative group work, students gain profound insights into concepts and develop vital skills for their future endeavors.

Beyond academic achievements, our secondary school experience emphasizes character education and social-emotional learning. We strive to instill values like integrity, empathy, resilience, and inclusivity in our students, shaping them into compassionate and responsible members of society. Through daily interactions, classroom dialogues, and service-oriented initiatives, students learn the significance of kindness, respect, and diversity.

At the core of our secondary school community is a dedicated cohort of educators who are passionate about inspiring and empowering every student to unlock their full potential. Our teachers are not only subject matter experts but also supportive mentors and role models who go the extra mile to nurture the academic, social, and emotional growth of each student.

Moreover, our secondary school is committed to fostering a culture of collaboration and partnership among students, parents, teachers, and the broader community. We believe that education is a collective endeavor, and through collaborative efforts, we create an inclusive and supportive learning environment where every student can thrive.

As we embark on this collective journey, we invite you to join us in embracing the joy of learning, the power of curiosity, and the richness of diversity. Together, let us inspire and empower the next generation of leaders, innovators, and changemakers who will positively impact the world.


Amarachi Onouha| Head of Secondary School
